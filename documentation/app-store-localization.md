# App Store Localizations — Memento Calendar

## How to Use

Copy each section directly into App Store Connect for the corresponding language. Character counts are shown as (actual/limit) with ✅ PASS or ❌ FAIL indicators. All fields marked ❌ must be shortened before submission.

**Notes**:
- CJK characters (Chinese, Japanese, Korean) are counted at face value; App Store Connect may weight them differently. Keep CJK fields conservatively under limit.
- Arabic text is right-to-left (RTL). Copy directly — App Store Connect will render correctly.
- Keywords must be comma-separated with no spaces after commas to stay within the 100-character limit.
- Promotional Text can be updated without a new app review submission.
