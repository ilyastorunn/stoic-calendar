## Description (4000 characters max)

```
See your time. Every day as a square. Filled or empty. Past or future.

Memento Calendar turns time into something you can see. Watch each day fill in on a visual grid. Year, month, week, or any custom period -- all at a glance.

WHY A VISUAL GRID?

A progress bar tells you a number. A grid shows you every single day. You see the weight of time passed and the space that remains. It changes how you think about your day.

YEAR VIEW
365 squares. One for each day. Watch 2026 fill in, square by square. See exactly where you stand -- not as a percentage, but as a pattern.

MONTH VIEW
30 squares for the current month. See your weeks take shape. Perfect for monthly goals, deadlines, or simply noticing how fast a month moves.

WEEK VIEW
7 squares, Monday to Sunday. Auto-resets each week. A simple check: how am I using this week?

CUSTOM TIMELINES
Track any period. 90-day challenges. Project deadlines. Time since quitting a habit. Days until a milestone. Create as many as you need.

HOME SCREEN WIDGETS

Your most important timeline, always visible.

Year Progress Widget -- watch 2026 fill up on your home screen. Small, medium, or large sizes.

Life in Weeks -- inspired by the viral Wait But Why concept. See your entire life on one screen. If you live 80 years, that is roughly 4,000 weeks.

Custom Timeline Widgets -- put any timeline on your home screen. Multiple widget instances supported.

Widgets update throughout the day. Multiple design styles available.

BUILT ON STOIC PHILOSOPHY

Marcus Aurelius wrote: "You could leave life right now. Let that determine what you do and say and think."

This is not morbid. It is clarifying. When you see time visually, each day becomes real. Procrastination fades. Priorities sharpen.

Memento mori -- remember that you will die -- is the oldest productivity tool. This app makes it visual.

PRIVACY FIRST

No account required. No sign-up. No personal data collected. No ads. No tracking. Everything stored locally on your device. Your time data never leaves your phone.

FREE & PREMIUM

Free: Unlimited timelines. All timeline types. Full grid visualization. Complete app functionality.

Premium: All home screen widgets. Multiple widget sizes and styles. Premium design themes. Exclusive color palettes. Priority support.

$3.99/month or $34.99/year. Payment charged to iTunes at purchase confirmation. Auto-renews unless turned off 24 hours before period ends. Manage subscriptions in Account Settings.

DESIGNED WITH CARE

Built by an independent developer. No gamification. No streaks. No dark patterns. No notifications. Just a clean, beautiful tool for seeing time clearly.

Works completely offline. Dark mode by default.
```
