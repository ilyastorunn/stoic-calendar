## Why Respond to Reviews?

- Apple shows developer responses publicly (builds trust)
- Responding to negative reviews can prompt users to update their rating
- Shows active development and care

## Review Response Guidelines

1. Respond within 24-48 hours of receiving the review
2. Keep responses under 3 sentences
3. Never argue with a reviewer
4. Always thank them regardless of rating
5. For bugs, offer a direct contact method
6. Personalize when possible (reference specific things they mentioned)
7. Never copy-paste the same response to multiple reviews (Apple may flag this)
