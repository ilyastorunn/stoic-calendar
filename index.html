What it does

Designed to make you pause,
not to keep you scrolling.

Year · Month · Week

Create timelines for any span of time. This year's 365 days. This month's grid. This week's seven squares. Or any custom range you define.

Grid Visualization

Every day is one dot. Filled dots are days gone. Empty ones are days remaining. Visual simplicity turns an abstraction into something visceral.

Share & Remind

Share your timeline as an image. Set a daily reminder to check in with the passing time — a gentle nudge, nothing more.

Private by Default

All data stays on your device. No account needed, no cloud sync, no analytics that profile you.

iOS Widget

Put the grid directly on your home screen. The year's progress, visible every time you unlock your phone.
